The Golden Rule

When roles stay separate, you're protected.

Your agent wants to make the deal. Your lawyer wants to protect you. Your financial advisor wants to optimize taxes. These are different jobs — hold them by different people. Mixing roles = conflicts of interest. Conflicts of interest = bad decisions.
