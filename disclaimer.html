What We Are

The Athlete Circle ("TAC") publishes educational content — including free guides, the $99 "Build Your Athlete Circle" ebook, strategy frameworks, scorecards, and consulting conversations — designed to help student-athletes and their families make better-informed decisions about their athletic careers.

Our content is built from publicly available information, real-world experience, and structured frameworks for decision-making. We do not represent athletes, negotiate contracts, source NIL deals, manage money, or provide legal opinions.

NIL Compliance Is Your Responsibility

NIL rules vary by state, NCAA conference, and individual school. You are responsible for complying with all applicable rules and policies, including but not limited to:

  • Your school's NIL compliance policy and disclosure requirements.
  • The NCAA's NIL policy and any conference overlay rules.
  • State NIL statutes where you reside and where you compete.
  • The Uniform Athlete Agents Act (UAA) where applicable.
  • Tax obligations (federal and state) on any NIL income.

TAC content is general and educational. We do not represent that any framework, template, or recommendation will be compliant in your specific jurisdiction or institution. Always confirm with your school's compliance office and your professional advisors.
